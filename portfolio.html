MIDI-Piano

A Unity project that plays MIDI files dynamically on a virtual piano with moving keys. The first step is parsing the input MIDI file and extracting its infomation, such as the speed, frequency, and duration of each note. Then, the project uses advanced and low-level utilization of Unity's AudioSource Component to play sine waves at the frequency of the corresponding note. Finally, at the press of each note, a grand piano rendered in the HDRP, that was custom modeled in Blender reacts to the press of the note, responding to factors such as the volume of the note, the pitch of the note, and how hard the note was pressed.
